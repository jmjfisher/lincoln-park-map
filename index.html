This application will help you navigate Manitowoc Lincoln Park!

The park offers tennis courts, a softball field, a zoo, plenty of parking, and cabins to rent for your upcoming events. Click to drop a pin and use the Search function to find which of these amenities you desire is nearest to you.

Notice something wrong? Have a question or suggestion? You may also leave a note for the Parks and Recreation Department at a pin's location using the Comment function. Click the Home button at any time to reset the map to its original state.

No matter what you're here for, enjoy your time in the park!
